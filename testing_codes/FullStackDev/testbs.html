Curate your culinary journeys with the help of local foodies

For the Foodies, by the foodies and to the foodies

At Talking Street, we believe that food is one of the best ways to get an insight into culture. Eating, talking and lingering across several places give us a different perspective that enriches us much more than our physical tummies.

A platform for foodies, by the foodies, we at Talking Street curate the most loved food recommendations by our local food experts across cities and towns. As a place that solely depends on your experience, we highly value local foodies who are willing to share their list of some of the best places to eat in and around you.

Be it crispy dosas from Chennai, sweet kulfis from Lucknow or the liveliest food from Singapore, we want to read all about it. We’re expanding across geographies and looking to cover everything from big cities to upcoming travel destinations (like Shillong, for example) in India, and are also on the path to exploring food in South-east Asia.
